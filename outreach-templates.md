## Usage Guidelines

1. **Personalization is Key:** Always research the recipient and customize each email
2. **Lead with Value:** Focus on what you can provide, not what you want
3. **Be Specific:** Include exact data, examples, and concrete offerings
4. **Professional Tone:** Maintain credibility while being approachable
5. **Clear Call-to-Action:** Make it easy for them to say yes
6. **Follow-up Appropriately:** 2-3 touches maximum, then move on
7. **Track Everything:** Monitor open rates, responses, and conversions

These templates should be adapted for each specific outreach opportunity while maintaining the core message and value proposition.
